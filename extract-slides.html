📸 Extract Slide Photos

This tool downloads your motorsport photos from Instagram as local JPEG files. Once saved in the slides/ folder, the homepage slideshow will use them permanently — no internet or Instagram login required.

Step 1 — Make sure you are logged into Instagram

Open instagram.com in this browser and confirm you are logged in as @realtape or @realiracing.

Step 2 — Download all 7 slide photos

Click the button below. Your browser will download 7 JPEG files. Then move them into the slides/ folder next to index.html.

Step 3 — Reload the homepage

Open index.html in your browser. The slideshow will now load your local photos instantly — no login needed.
